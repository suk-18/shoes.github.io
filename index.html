Shoes Designer

A shoe designer is a type of fashion designer who focuses solely on creating footwear. A shoe designer can specialize in different footwear, such as sneakers, high-end heels, sandals, cold-weather shoes, athletic footwear and other specialty footwear.

There are several education requirements to become a shoe designer. Shoe designers usually study graphic design, business or psychology. 61% of shoe designers hold a bachelor's degree and 15% hold a associate degree
